Attacking

Each round is 6 seconds long.

To attack, roll to hit: roll 1d20 + the attribute modifier for the weapon + the target's armour class + shooter's attack bonus for their level + weapon modifiers

Disengaging from close combat without taking an action to do so gives an attack of opportunity

Combat

Character can act and move up to 20m. Actions include making attacks, reloading, operating devices or moving an additional 20m. Free actions can be done whenever.

Characters may delay actions to respond to event.

Encumbrance

May have number of items ready equal to half STR score, which can be drawn as part of action. Can have number of stowed items equal to their STR score which takes a round to ready. 100 credits, 6 Type-A batteries or 3 magazines take up one slot.

2 additional items, can be added but they become Lightly Encumbered: can move 15m, which can be doubled again to 10m movement.

Extended Skill Checks

Make several skill checks until they get a certain number of successes.

Hazards

Hard Vacuum - first round takes no damage, then takes 1d20 each round with Physical Effect save for half.

Falling - 1d6 damage per 3 meters. Luck saving throw saves half.

Radiation - On failed Physical Effects save, a day later character loses 1 point of Constitution from effects which can only be cured at TL4 medical facility for 48 hours. Medkits won't work.

Suffocation - characters can go without air for (4 + CONS modifier) minutes of air, which is halved for something strenuous. Afterwards, they make Physical Effects save or lose consciousness.

Healing

When a character is reduced to 0 hit points or less, they fall unconscious, losing 1 HP until they reach -10, when they die.

A character can be stabilised with a Lazarus Patch or a Biopsionics psychic.

After being stabilised Biopscionics can revive them. Otherwise, they require 1d4 days of treatment before they gain 1 HP and heal normally. With TL3 it's 1d4 months which is the same for TL2, but they must make Physical Effects save with attending medic's Tech/Medical skill, a failure cancels the process.

Every day, a character gains HP equal to his level when he wakes up. He gains that again if he relaxes for the whole day, with two more for every level of Tech/Medical possessed by the attending physician.

Movement

If a character moves away from an enemy without disengaging as their action they get an attack of opportunity.

Can move 20m, up to 40m if they give up their action.

Saving Throws

Physical Effect - resist diseases or poisons

Mental Effect - psionic powers or mind alterations

Evasion - things that can be jumped away from

Tech - alien beam weapons, nanite infections etc

Luck - dumb luck

Space Ship Combat

Pilots add their ship's SPE to any Vehicle/Space tests. Armour is subtracted from incoming damage. If ship is taken to 0 HP for non-fighters, chief engineer makes Tech/Astronautics at Diff8, success means a helpless husk with no functioning systems; failure means explodes in 3d6 rounds.

To hit, roll 1d20 + Gunner's AB + Combat/Gunnery skill + Attribute Modifier + target's Armour. If greater than 20, it's a hit!

Space Ship Maintanence and Repair

At least 100 credits a day per crew for payroll.

Must be refuelled after each drill with Hydrogen. Costs 200 credits per drive rating and takes 6 hours.

Maintenance costs 5% of the hull's base price each year (2,083creds a month for The Noble Reproach). After a year, they get -1 to all tests. Once the debt is half the ship's base, it can't fly.

It costs 1,000 crews per HP to heal The Noble Reproach.

Space Ship Travel

From planet to moon is 6 hours, from one region to another is 48. This is divided by the Spike level of the drive.

Each jump takes an hour of prep per hex, and can be made up to 1 hex for each Spike level, and takes 6 days divided by Spike Level.

Jumping between stars requires Navigation(1) on Intelligence against diff 7. This is increased by the following factors:

Uncharted course +4

Charts older than 5 years +2

Charts between 1 and 5 years old +1

Jump, per two full hexes +1

Course Trimming per level +1

Course Trimming is being more daring, adding one difficulty per level allowing for an increase in Spike level. Can't increase more than Navigation rating.
